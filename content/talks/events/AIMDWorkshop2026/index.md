# AIMD NeuroTech Bootcamp 2026

**A hands-on introduction to neural prosthetics — the evening before the AIMD Workshop.**

The NeuroTech Bootcamp is a pre-event to the [Active Implantable Medical Devices (AIMD) Workshop 2026](https://www.aanmelder.nl/aimdworkshop2026/home), organized by **NeuroTechHub**. In a single interactive evening, participants get a guided tour of the full neural prosthetics pipeline — from **neural data acquisition** and **signal processing** to **decoding**, **modeling**, and **closed-loop control** — using the Python workflows, machine-learning libraries, and simulation tools that are standard across academia and industry.

## When & where

- **Date:** Wednesday, 3 June 2026
- **Time:** 17:30 – 22:15 CEST
- **Venue:** [Pulse, TU Delft campus](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pulse+Landbergstraat+19+Delft)
- **Address:** Landbergstraat 19, 2628 CE Delft (between the Industrial Design Engineering and Mechanical Engineering faculties)

## What to expect

- **Guided introduction** to the neural prosthetics pipeline
- **Hands-on programming exercises** in small groups, working through real-world neural-prosthetics challenges
- **Python workflows** and the ML & simulation tools used in the field
- **Networking** with fellow participants ahead of the AIMD Workshop

## Who is this for?

Students and early-career researchers with **basic programming experience**. No prior neurotechnology expertise is required — just curiosity and a laptop.

## How it connects to the AIMD Workshop

The bootcamp runs on the evening of **3 June**, directly before the two-day **AIMD Workshop 2026** (4–5 June) at the Aula Congress Centre, TU Delft. You can either **add the bootcamp to your AIMD Workshop ticket** for an extra **€10** during workshop registration, or **register for the bootcamp on its own** if you're only joining for the evening.
